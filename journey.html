Three years ago, I decided to step out of my comfort zone and open a new chapter of my life when I go to college. At first, I study business admistration because my family does business, and they want me to follow their path. So I just listen to my parents and study business administration for about one years. Eventhough I was interested in commerce and business, I really did not feel fit in. I felt like there was something wrong, and it was not my passion. I shared my feeling to my parents, and they said no. They wanted me to do business because that's what they have done their whole life and it is easier for me to follow it their ways. One day, my friend asked me to joined in a coding workshop with her. After the workshop, I was just fascinated and amazed by the coding itselt, and I asked myself if I could spend endless hours on programming a program without feeling exhausted. The answer is YES! So, I decided to change the major to computer science even with the disagreement of my parents. My mother prevented me from switching my major because she thought that women could not do coding, and especially she thought that how a little girl at the age of nineteen with zero-experience could compete with somebody doing coding since they were ten. I told her that I would prove it to her. I am determined that I can do it, and if I program hard enough in a long term, I could definitely be a professional in this field, and make my own way into the real word. Since then, I have experienced in programming by working on some schools and personal projects for about two years.

Another important reseason for me to purse programming is that I have a very big passion to work in a colloborative and productive environment.
