Here is the story begins....

This is a picture of me and my grandma when I graduated from my commmunity college with an A.A Degree

Three years ago, I decided to step out of my comfort zone and opened a new chapter of my life when I went to college. At first, I studied business admistration because my family did business, and they wantted me to follow their path. So I just listened to my parents and studied business administration for about one years. Eventhough I was interested in commerce and business, I really did not feel it was my passion. I felt like there was something wrong. I shared my feeling to my parents, and they said no. They wanted me to do business than something else because that's what they have done their whole life and it woule be easier for me to follow their ways.


One day, my friend asked me to joined in a coding workshop with her. After the workshop, I was just fascinated and amazed by how programming could create incredible things, and I asked myself if I could spend endless hours on coding without feeling exhausted. The answer was YES! So, I decided to change the major to computer science even with the disagreement of my parents. My mother prevented me from switching my major because she thought that women could not do coding, and particularly she thought that how a little girl at the age of nineteen with zero experience could compete with somebody learning coding since they were ten. I told her that I would prove it to her. I am determined that I can do it, and if I put effort into it in a long term, I could definitely make my own way into this field and become a professional. Since then, I have experienced in programming by working on some schools and personal projects for about two years.


Another important reseason for me to purse programming is that I have a very big passion to work in a colloborative and productive environment. I always want to be a part of a potential team and develop incredible things with my team members. I believe that teamwork and talent are the most important keys to create wonderful things. And my dream is to be able to develop amazing things that will significantly improve the learning, working, and health of people with my team members.
